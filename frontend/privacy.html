Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SoloAct ("we", "us") is operated by Cryan Advisory Ltd, an Irish company. SoloAct is a music education tool that helps you understand and play the chords in your AI-generated songs. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights under GDPR.

What we collect

Account data: when you create an account, we collect your email address and (if you use Google sign-in) your name as provided by Google. This is stored securely in our authentication system.

Payment data: billing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details — Stripe processes and stores payment information on our behalf. We retain a Stripe customer ID and subscription status in our database to manage your access.

Chord charts: if you save a chord chart, we store the analysis result (key, chords, tempo, title) linked to your account. We do not store the audio file — it is processed server-side and deleted immediately.

Usage data: we store a count of analyses in your browser's localStorage to enforce the one free analysis limit for anonymous users. This is not sent to our servers and contains no personal data.

Why we collect it

We collect account and payment data to provide the subscription service and manage your access. We collect saved chart data so you can access your charts from any device. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Lawful basis (GDPR)

We process your data on the basis of contract — processing is necessary to provide the service you have subscribed to. For optional email communications, we rely on consent.

How long we keep it

We retain your account data and saved charts for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is deleted within 30 days. Payment records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and tax compliance.

Third-party processors

Supabase (supabase.com) — authentication and database. Stores your account details and saved charts. Data is hosted in the EU. See supabase.com/privacy.

Stripe (stripe.com) — payment processing. Handles all billing and card data. See stripe.com/privacy.

Resend (resend.com) — transactional email. Used to send sign-in links and account notifications. See resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Railway (railway.app) — application hosting. Audio files are temporarily processed on Railway's infrastructure and immediately deleted after analysis.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to: access the data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request erasure ("right to be forgotten"); object to processing; withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@soloact.app.

Browser storage

We use localStorage in your browser to track anonymous analysis count and store your authentication session. This data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: info@soloact.app
