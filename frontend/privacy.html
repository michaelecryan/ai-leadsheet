Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SoloAct ("we", "us") is a music education tool that helps you understand and play the chords in your AI-generated songs. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights.

What we collect

The only personal data we collect is your email address, and only if you choose to submit it via the "notify me at launch" form. We do not collect names, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

When you upload an audio file, it is processed server-side to detect chords and is deleted immediately after processing. We do not store your audio files.

Why we collect it

We collect your email address solely to notify you when SoloAct officially launches. We will not use it for any other purpose, and we will not share it with third parties for marketing.

Lawful basis (GDPR)

We process your email address on the basis of consent — you gave it to us voluntarily by submitting the form. You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

How long we keep it

We will keep your email address until SoloAct launches and for a reasonable period afterwards (up to 12 months). You can ask us to delete it at any time.

Third-party processors

We use Resend (resend.com) to store contact information and send emails. Resend acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can review their privacy policy at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Our application is hosted on Railway (railway.app). Audio files you upload are temporarily processed on Railway's infrastructure and immediately deleted.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to: access the data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request erasure ("right to be forgotten"); withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@soloact.app.

Cookies

We use a single item in your browser's localStorage to remember that you've already seen the email capture prompt. This is not a tracking cookie — it contains no personal data and is never sent to our servers.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: info@soloact.app
